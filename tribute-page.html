  • 1968 - Born in Tombel, South West Region Kumba

  • 1985 - Had his GCE ordinary level Certificate from GHS Nyassoso
  • 1987 - Had his GCE advanced level Certificate from CCAS Bambili
  • 1990 - Graduated from the higher teachers training college ENS Bamenda
  • 1994 - Posted to teach in GBHS Mamfe where he started his teaching career as Chemistry teacher
  • 2019 - Dies at the age of 51
